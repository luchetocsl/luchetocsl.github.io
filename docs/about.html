Hola!

I am Luis Martinez. I am a Mexican designer living in Ottawa.

I am a Product Designer and Front-End Developer with over ten years of experience helping small and medium B2C businesses to plan, design, and develop their websites.

As a former business owner, I have strong business sense and marketing knowledge, which I use to make design decisions that move the needle and impact the bottom line.

I have collaborated with designers, developers, and clients to streamline product design projects, bringing ideas to market faster and at a lower cost. My experience communicating with designers, developers, and clients translates into more streamlined projects.

I am seeking a full-time remote or hybrid opportunity as a Product Designer at a small to mid-size studio where my unique approach to designing and coding could have a meaningful impact.
